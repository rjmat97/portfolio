My passions Include:

Art

I dont believe in confining the term "art" to just sketching or painting. Anything done with a pinch of creativity, I believe, can be called art.

Some of my art include sketches (most of with is abstract or human body poses) and the ocassional music I create when I just can't control my levels of excitement (most of which is derived from the rain and cold weather)

Photography

I like to think of myself as an artist before I think of myself as a photographer, simply beacuse of the nature of the images I like capturing. So far my expression in terms of photography has always heavily depended on how far i could push my images in post rocessing.

Following common norms is something I strongly deslike, but as my passion grows everyday, I see myself making an effort to keep my images simple and original, as far as possible.

Tech

Everything from tinkering with android ROMs to arduino micro controllers and single board compters. If it runs on electricity, you have my undivided attention.

This passion is mainly fueled by my need to customise and tinker with every little thing i can lay my hands on... and if it's fixeable, why should I prevent myself from doing it myself?
